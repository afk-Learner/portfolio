About Me

Who am I?

I'm Samarth Sapare — a student and an animator driven by passion and purpose. As a kid, anime opened my eyes to worlds where emotion, creativity, and imagination collided. That spark grew into a deep love for storytelling through motion. Now, every frame I create is more than animation—it's a way of giving life to ideas and honoring the art that first inspired me. Alongside this, I'm on a journey of self-improvement—constantly learning, growing, and refining both my craft and my character. My work reflects not just what I create, but who I'm becoming.
